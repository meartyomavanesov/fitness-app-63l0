The Fitness app is designed for individuals seeking to improve their physical health and fitness levels. It provides users with tools to track workouts, monitor progress, and set fitness goals tailored to their personal needs. Users can log their exercises, view performance metrics, and receive insights into their fitness journey. This app benefits users by promoting accountability, encouraging consistency, and helping them achieve their health objectives.

- Workout tracking
- Progress monitoring
- Goal setting
- Exercise logging
- Performance metrics
- User insights
- Health objective support
- Personalized fitness plans
